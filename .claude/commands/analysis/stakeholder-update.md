# Update Stakeholder Context

Review recent transcripts and update stakeholder information.

## Steps

1. Read current `context/stakeholders.md`
2. Read the 5 most recent summaries from `analysis/summaries/`
3. Read the most recent weekly report

## Update Areas

### For each stakeholder mentioned in recent transcripts:

- **Role changes**: Has their title, responsibility, or scope changed?
- **Sentiment shift**: Are they more/less engaged, frustrated, supportive?
- **New relationships**: Any new collaborations or tensions with other stakeholders?
- **Key positions**: Have they taken a notable stance on any topic?
- **Commitments**: Any new commitments or unfulfilled ones?
- **Influence**: Has their influence on the programme increased or decreased?

### New Stakeholders
- Anyone new appearing in transcripts who isn't in the stakeholder file?
- Add them with: name, role, first appearance date, initial assessment

### Departed Stakeholders
- Anyone who was active but hasn't appeared recently?
- Note when they were last active

## Output
Update `context/stakeholders.md` with changes.
Add dates to changes so the evolution is traceable.
Report what was updated and why.
