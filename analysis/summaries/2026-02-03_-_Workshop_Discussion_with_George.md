## Narrative Notes
This short call packs significant strategic value. George's distinction between CLARA (IRP migration) and Gainsight (overall customer health) is the single most important positioning decision the programme has made since launch. It gives the team a defensible story: CLARA is a small, specific tool for a specific programme, not a shadow replacement for the enterprise customer success platform. This framing was later adopted as the official line when the Gainsight team started asking questions. George's insistence on a co-presenter and action-oriented format also shows his understanding that top-down demos create passivity, while peer-led hands-on sessions create ownership. If the workshop succeeds, it will be because of the principles laid down in this 13-minute conversation.
