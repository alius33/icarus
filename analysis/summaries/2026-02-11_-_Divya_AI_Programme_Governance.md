# Meeting with Divya on AI Programme Governance
**Date:** 2026-02-11
**Attendees:** Divya (Speaker 1), Idrees, Richard Dosoo, Azmain Hossain
**Duration context:** Short-medium (~17 minutes)
**Workstreams touched:** WS2 CLARA, WS3 CS Agent (Sales Recon), Programme Governance

## Key Points
- This is a pivotal alignment session. Richard and Idrees are briefing Divya on the full context of what the insurance CS team has been doing with AI tooling.
- Idrees provides the essential backstory for Divya: the RMS acquisition created a gap where insurance CS does not have Gainsight working. Charlotte's team is launching MA 3.0 in March for existing OUs, but RMS data is not included. Because of this gap, Richard's team proactively built CLARA using Cursor — described by Idrees as impressive work that should not be downplayed.
- The Sales Recon pilot launched today with 8 CSMs. The pilot will feed requirements into Anna's team (on Jamie's roadmap) who are building CS-specific features. Jamie is supportive but his team is small and does not have a mandate to build a tool specifically for the insurance group.
- Divya asks three key questions that will shape the programme's trajectory: (1) What are the efficiency metrics/KPIs? She wants to measure ROI and prove the hypothesis that AI is helping. (2) Is Sales Recon the biggest bang for the buck, or are there other AI solutions to consider? She mentions customer onboarding as an example — possibly using tools like Anthropic's or OpenAI's agent kit. (3) She wants next week's discussion to be "philosophical" about priorities rather than diving into specifics.
- Divya raises the importance of not duplicating efforts. She asks whether Dominic's team (doing retro churn analysis) is aligned with the forward-looking work Richard and Idrees are doing. Idrees explains: Dominic's team looks backward to identify churn drivers; their team looks forward to prevent churn. Cyril from Dominic's team is included in the Sales Recon pilot to stay aligned.
- Richard offers to provide "directional KPIs" about the projects they are working on to help drive next week's discussion.
- Divya frames the strategic question: the company is going through headcount reductions that impact service quality. How can AI bridge that gap? This lens will be applied to all AI investments.
- Idrees claims 15-30 hours per week savings per CSM through the initiatives. He has two CSMs in the Sales Recon pilot who will provide before/after comparison data. Charlotte's Gainsight launch in March will provide activity-level tracking for measurement.
